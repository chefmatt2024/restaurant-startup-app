üìã Firebase Console Setup Instructions

Step 1: Access Firebase Console

Go to https://console.firebase.google.com/

Select your project: restaurant-startup-app

Step 2: Enable Email/Password Authentication

  1. Click Authentication in the left sidebar
  2. Click Sign-in method tab
  3. Find Email/Password and click on it
  4. Toggle Enable to ON
  5. Click Save

Step 3: Enable Google Authentication

  1. In the same Sign-in method tab
  2. Find Google and click on it
  3. Toggle Enable to ON
  4. Set Project support email (your email)
  5. Click Save

Step 4: Add Authorized Domains

  1. Go to Authentication ‚Üí Settings tab
  2. Scroll to Authorized domains
  3. Add: localhost
  4. Click Add domain

Step 5: Verify Configuration
