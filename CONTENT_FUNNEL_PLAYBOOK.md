# Content Funnel Playbook (Iterum Foods -> Restauranteur App)

This playbook is designed to bring new restaurant owners, buyers, and operators into the website, convert them with the free assessment, and move them into trial and paid plans.

**See also:** [Outlets & repost workflow](content-kit/OUTLETS_AND_REPOST_WORKFLOW.md) — where to start (create once), where to repost (order), and where the funnel ends.

## Goal

- Increase qualified website traffic
- Convert traffic into assessment completions
- Convert assessments into signups and subscriptions

## Primary ICP Segments

- First-time restaurant founders
- Existing owners evaluating expansion/acquisition
- Buyers doing due diligence before purchase

## Funnel Structure

### TOFU (Attract)

Purpose: bring the right people to `iterumfoods.xyz`.

Content types:
- SEO blog posts (problem/intent-driven)
- Short social videos (30-60 sec tips)
- Downloadable checklists (light lead magnet)
- Partner guest posts/newsletters

Core TOFU topics:
- "How to evaluate a restaurant before buying"
- "Restaurant startup mistakes to avoid"
- "Restaurant due diligence checklist"
- "What permits do you need to open a restaurant in MA"

CTA:
- "Take the Free Restaurant Assessment"

### MOFU (Engage + Qualify)

Purpose: capture intent and qualify prospects.

Content types:
- Free assessment landing page
- Results interpretation content ("What your score means")
- Email mini-course (5 emails)
- Comparison guides/templates

Core MOFU assets:
- Assessment completion page with next-step recommendations
- "How to improve your score by 20 points" guide
- Case-style breakdowns (good vs risky acquisition examples)

CTA:
- "Sign up free to save and expand your due diligence plan"

### BOFU (Convert)

Purpose: turn engaged users into trial and paid users.

Content types:
- Product walkthrough demos
- ROI and outcome pages
- Pricing FAQ / objection handling
- Limited-time onboarding offer

Core BOFU assets:
- 3-minute product tour video
- "From assessment to investor-ready plan in 7 days"
- "Why owners choose Iterum vs spreadsheets/consultants"

CTA:
- "Start free trial"
- "Book implementation call"

## 30-Day Content Plan

Week 1:
- Publish 2 SEO posts (due diligence + permits)
- Publish 3 short videos repurposed from post snippets
- Add assessment CTA block to all major pages

Week 2:
- Publish 1 case-style post + 1 checklist post
- Launch 5-email nurture sequence for assessment leads
- Add retargeting ads for assessment non-completers

Week 3:
- Publish 1 product-tour article + 1 ROI article
- Host one live Q&A/webinar for buyers/founders
- Launch partner outreach with tracked links

Week 4:
- Double down on top-performing channels
- Refresh underperforming headlines/CTAs
- Publish monthly benchmark recap ("restaurant risk trends")

## Email Nurture Sequence (Assessment Leads)

Email 1 (Day 0): Results recap + top 3 risks to fix  
Email 2 (Day 2): Permit/financial red flags checklist  
Email 3 (Day 4): Mini case study (before/after)  
Email 4 (Day 6): Product walkthrough + setup checklist  
Email 5 (Day 8): Trial offer + urgency CTA

Primary CTA in each email:
- "Start free trial and save your full due diligence plan"

## Distribution Channels

- Organic search (SEO pages + internal links)
- **LinkedIn company page:** [Iterum Foods LLC](https://www.linkedin.com/company/iterum-foods-llc/) — company updates, tips, and CTAs to assessment/trial; use UTM `utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=company_page`. See [content-kit/LINKEDIN_COMPANY_PAGE.md](content-kit/LINKEDIN_COMPANY_PAGE.md) for copy and improvements.
- YouTube Shorts / Instagram Reels / LinkedIn clips
- Email list + partner newsletters
- Local founder communities and associations
- Paid search for high-intent keywords

## Event Tracking Requirements

Track these funnel events consistently:
- `organic_search_click`
- `social_click`
- `partner_referral_click`
- `email_click`
- `landing_viewed`
- `cta_click_assessment`
- `assessment_started`
- `assessment_completed`
- `signup_completed`
- `trial_started`
- `subscription_started`

Required attribution params:
- `utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign`, `utm_content`
- `first_touch_source`, `last_touch_source`
- `referral_partner_id` (when applicable)

## KPI Targets (First 60 Days)

- Assessment start rate: >= 12% of landing visitors
- Assessment completion rate: >= 55% of assessment starts
- Signup rate from assessment completions: >= 18%
- Trial-to-paid conversion: >= 12%
- Paid CAC payback: <= 3 months

## Governance

- Publish at least 2 TOFU + 1 MOFU + 1 BOFU asset per week
- Weekly funnel review: traffic -> assessment -> signup -> paid
- Kill or rewrite assets with weak CTR after 2 weeks
- Scale channels with best qualified conversion, not vanity traffic
