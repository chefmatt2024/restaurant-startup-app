## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Week 1-2)
**Goal:** Test with small group, gather feedback, fix critical issues

#### Actions:
1. **Beta Testing Program**
   - Invite 10-20 restaurant entrepreneurs
   - Collect feedback via in-app feedback tool
   - Monitor analytics for usage patterns
   - Fix critical bugs and UX issues

2. **Content Preparation**
   - Write 3-5 blog posts about restaurant planning
   - Create social media content calendar
   - Prepare email sequences

3. **SEO Foundation**
   - Optimize meta tags and descriptions
   - Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
   - Set up Google Analytics and Search Console

### Phase 2: Public Launch (Week 3-4)
**Goal:** Drive initial traffic and conversions

#### Marketing Channels:

**1. Content Marketing**
- **Blog Topics:**

  - "Financial Projections: What Investors Want to See"

  - "How to Choose the Perfect Restaurant Location"

- **SEO Keywords:**
  - restaurant business plan
  - restaurant financial projections
  - restaurant startup costs
  - how to open a restaurant
  - restaurant business plan template

**2. Social Media**
- **Platforms:** LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X
- **Content Types:**
  - Tips and insights
  - Success stories
  - Behind-the-scenes
  - User testimonials
  - Free resources

**3. Email Marketing**
- **Welcome Series** (5 emails over 2 weeks)
  1. Welcome + Getting Started Guide
