## Quick Setup (5 minutes)

### For Project Owners:

1. **Share a Project**
   - Open Draft Manager (click draft name in header)
   - Click the Share icon (ðŸ“¤) next to any draft
   - Enter collaborator's email
   - Choose permission (Viewer or Editor)
   - Click "Send Invite"

2. **Manage Collaborators**
   - View all collaborators in Share modal
   - Change permissions anytime
   - Remove collaborators if needed

### For Collaborators:

1. **Accept Invitation**
   - Look for bell icon (ðŸ””) in header
   - Click to see pending invitations
   - Click "Accept" to gain access
   - Project appears in your draft list

2. **Work on Shared Project**
   - Open the shared project from draft list
   - Make changes (if Editor)
   - Changes sync automatically

## Permission Levels

- **Viewer**: Read-only, perfect for investors/reviewers
- **Editor**: Can edit, perfect for team members/consultants
- **Owner**: Full control (you, the creator)

## Common Workflows

### Team Collaboration
1. Owner shares with team members (Editor access)
2. Team members accept invitations
3. Everyone works on the same project
4. Changes sync in real-time

### Consultant Review
1. Owner shares with consultant (Editor access)
2. Consultant accepts and reviews
3. Consultant makes suggestions directly
4. Owner reviews and approves changes

### Investor Presentation
1. Owner shares with investors (Viewer access)
2. Investors can review without making changes
3. Professional, secure presentation
